💳 How Payment Works

All fees are paid by bank transfer to the instructor's registered account. Before any commitment is finalised:

1. TF issues a formal Letter of Engagement (scope, dates, agreed fee)

2. Instructor confirms via signed letter or email acceptance

3. Bank details collected securely via TF Operations

4. Payment released within 5 working days of session delivery

5. Payment receipt and confirmation sent by email

Mentors are paid on the 1st of each internship month (months 4, 5, 6).

📌 Rate Factors Considered

  • Years of experience (7–10 yrs vs 15+ yrs)
  • Seniority (Manager vs Director vs CHRO)
  • Module complexity and preparation required
  • Physical vs virtual delivery (physical earns premium)
  • Whether new content or existing material is used
  • Combined tutor + mentor role (bundle premium)

Rates are agreed privately. No published rate card. This protects flexibility and treats each instructor as an individual, not a line item.
